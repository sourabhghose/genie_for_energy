# Emissions Definitions: Scope 1, 2, 3

GHG Protocol definitions for corporate greenhouse gas accounting.

## Scope 1 — Direct Emissions

Emissions from sources owned or controlled by the organization.

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Stationary combustion | Boilers, generators, backup diesel |
| Mobile combustion | Fleet vehicles, construction equipment |
| Process emissions | Industrial processes |
| Fugitive emissions | Refrigerants, methane leaks |

**For retail energy providers:** Typically limited (no generation); may include fleet, buildings, backup generators.

---

## Scope 2 — Indirect Emissions (Purchased Energy)

Emissions from the generation of purchased electricity, steam, heating, and cooling consumed by the organization.

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Purchased electricity | Grid electricity for offices, facilities |
| Purchased steam/heat | District heating |
| Purchased cooling | Chilled water |

**For retail energy providers:** 
- Own operations: office/facility electricity
- **Customer consumption** (optional): Often reported separately as "enabled emissions" or in Scope 3 Category 11

**Calculation:** `kWh × Emission Factor (kg CO2e/MWh) / 1000`

---

## Scope 3 — Other Indirect Emissions

Emissions in the value chain not included in Scope 1 or 2.

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| 1 | Purchased goods and services |
| 2 | Capital goods |
| 3 | Fuel- and energy-related activities |
| 4 | Upstream transportation |
| 5 | Waste |
| 6 | Business travel |
| 7 | Employee commuting |
| 8 | Upstream leased assets |
| 9 | Downstream transportation |
| 10 | Processing of sold products |
| 11 | Use of sold products |
| 12 | End-of-life treatment |
| 13 | Downstream leased assets |
| 14 | Franchises |
| 15 | Investments |

**For retail energy providers:** 
- **Category 11 (Use of sold products)** is material: emissions from electricity consumed by customers
- Category 3 (purchased electricity for resale) may apply

---

## Location-Based vs Market-Based (Scope 2)

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location-based** | Uses grid average emission factor (e.g., Australian NGA factors) for the geographic state |
| **Market-based** | Uses emission factors from contractual instruments (RECs, PPAs, tariffs) |

Most reports include both. Location-based reflects physical grid; market-based reflects procurement choices.
